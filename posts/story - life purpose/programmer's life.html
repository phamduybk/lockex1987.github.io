Life of a programmer.

Have you ever ask yourself: "What is the purpose of your life?"

I've spent almost haft of my life, and I still asking me the same question.

My career is programmer. So I focus on "life of a programmer".

I think create a useful software that people use it happily is a successful. NoMoney (Not Only Money :). Of course you must have money to live, but that is not the most important thing.

Earn money honestly and honorably.

Some people say that freelancers sell their value cheaply. Is it true?

I don't like to create some trivial applications like some games (or some trivial songs like "Gangnam style"). I don't criticize them, I just don't like them. They rise and gone.

I want some useful application like 'Pharmacy sale support system' for my sister.

I think freelance is an interesting idea. Free to do anything. If you works hard, you will earn more (you gain for you pain). No one manages you, ask you to do this or to do that. No pressure, no stress. Every new day is different from other. It's fun (or scared :) ) experience.

WORK WITH PASSION! NEVER GIVE UP!

Different company, different technology.

One compay. one technology.

Learn and work hard -> skilled programmer.
