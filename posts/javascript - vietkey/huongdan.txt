                      CONG CU TIM KIEM DANH CHO DAN NGHIEP DU

            =======================================

  Chuc nang: Bo tim kiem nay co the dung tim kiem trong website cung nhu ngoai Internet va da duoc nhung bo go tieng Viet nen ban co the su dung tieng Viet de tim kiem, nhap du lieu...
  Huong dan: Trong bo tim kiem nay co 5 file can chu y:
               + search.htm : Noi dat bo tim kiem va hien ket qua tim kiem...
               + style.css : File nay dung de trang tri cho cac text link trong bo tim kiem
               + vietuni.js : Bo go tieng Viet dung de nhup vao trang tim kiem
               + xsearch.js : Cac thong so can cho cong cu tim kiem, ban can phai xem xet ki cang, chi can sai mot chi tiet se lam cho bo tim kiem bi loi.
               + db.js : Nhap du lieu cho bo tim kem nhu: ten website, tu khoa, gioi thieu ve trang web...Ban can phai chu y truoc ten website phai co "http://" neu thieu khi tim thay thay trang web do se khong hien ra.
     Chu y: Bo tim kiem nay xem tot nhat o trinh duyet Internet Explorer 6.0, ban hay dat doan code sau day vao bat cu dau trong website va no se hien ket qua tai trang "search.htm" :
     <script language="JavaScript" src="js/vietuni.js" type='text/javascript'></script>
     <script language="Javascript" src="js/xsearch.js"></script>
     <script language="Javascript" src="js/db.js"></script>
     <script language="Javascript">
      initXsearch();</script>

     Chuc ban thanh cong !
