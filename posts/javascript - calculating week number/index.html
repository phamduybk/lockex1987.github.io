Calculating week numbers in JavaScript

My customers require we create a date picker that they can choose a week and navigate back and forth. The week is identified by date range (start date - end date) and week number. We have to calculate the week number from a date.

First week

The ISO 8601 definition for week 1 is the week with the year's fist Thursday in it. The following definitions based on properties of this week are mutually equivalent, since the ISO week starts with Monday:

  • It is the first week with a majority (4 or more) of its day in January
  • Its first day is the Monday nearest to 1 January
  • It has 4 January in it
  • It has the year's first working day in it, if Saturdays, Sundays and 1 January are not working days

How to get the week number from a date

The code below add two new functions to the Date object. To get the ISO week number (1-53) of a Date object, e.g myDate, use myDate.getWeek(). To get the corresponding four-digit year, use myDate.getWeekYear().
